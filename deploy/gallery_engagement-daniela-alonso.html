I had the pleasure of meeting Daniela and Alonso when they were in town to throw Alonso's dad a surprise birthday party. They drove all the way from Tucson!

We walked along the historic Venice Beach Boardwalk. No big deal.


Then, they had some ice cream. I watched.

After some more frollicking, we decided to end our beachy tour in Marina Del Rey. Yes, we frollicked far.

Then, since the sun was setting, we had to walk all the way back to our cars. We made it fun.

What a nice couple! I'm happy to say I'll be joining them on their wedding day in August 2012!
